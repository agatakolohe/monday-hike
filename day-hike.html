Monday August 10th Hike

My friend and I decided to hike in Mt. Rainierthis Monday. It was a beautiful and clear summer day, perfect conditions to observe the sleeping giant.

We spent four hours hiking a few of the trails in Paradise.

  • Skyline Trail
  • Myrtle Falls
  • Deadhouse Creek Trail
  • Alta Vista Trail

Places to go in Mt. Rainier National Park.

  1. Paradise is known for its wildflower meadows.
  2. Sunrise is the highest point you can drive to.

WARNING

BEWARE OF BEARS

Things to remember when sharing space with wildlife:

  • Pack and dispose food/trash properly.
  • DO NOT APPROACH WILD LIFE. Let and let live.
  • Pack essential equipment for activities:
    1. Water and food
    2. Sunscreen
    3. Bear Spray
    4. Map of area
    5. Cell phone/GPS
